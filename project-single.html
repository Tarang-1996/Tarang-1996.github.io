project Description

Park City is a 26-acre residential township located at Bareilly bypass road. It offers three types of residential units – Type A 162 yards, Type B 150 yards, Type C 114 yards. The township is designed to provide a close-to-nature feel to the residents. There are 18 parks, planned in such a manner that each house has one park in close proximity. The infrastructure such as roads is designed keeping future demand in mind, for smooth circulation. The township also boasts in-house facilities such as an open gym, yoga center and jogging tracks to promote a healthy lifestyle.
