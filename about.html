About Us

DG Infra Group was incorporated in 2009 with a vision to deliver affordable yet quality housing to its clients. The group is led by Mr. Dharmendra Gupta, who comes with more than three decades of experience in the Real Estate and Construction industry, and has delivered many projects in the housing sector in Bareilly and is constantly turning people’s dream homes into a reality. He is also the treasurer of RERA Bareilly Chapter.

Within a small span of time, DG Infra Group has experienced tremendous growth and success due to the timely delivery of its projects. Since its inception, the company’s reputation is built on benchmark quality standards, rock-solid values, uncompromising business ethos, a customer-centric approach, and above all transparency in all spheres of conducting business.The group is now catering to both affordable as well as luxury real estate segments with its various projects across the city
