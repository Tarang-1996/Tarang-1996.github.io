Who we are

DG Infra Build was incorporated in 2009 with a vision to deliver affordable yet quality housing to its clients. DG Infra Group and has multiple sister companies & partnership firms. With more than two decades of experience in Real Estate and Construction, Director Mr. Dharmendra Gupta has delivered many projects in affordable housing sector in Bareilly and is constantly turning people’s dream home into reality. Within a small span of time, DG Infra Group has experienced tremendous growth & success due to timely delivery of its projects. Since inception, the company’s reputation is built on benchmark quality standards, rock solid values, uncompromising business ethos, customer centric approach, above all transparency in all spheres of conducting business.
